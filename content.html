BAMBOOZLED VICTIM

What is DontBuythisNFT?


Our NFT is a unique opportunity to invest in one of 10,000 individual Nfts and become part of our unique "Bamboozled Victim" community. This nft was created to educate people who are new to NFTs and show them the specific opportunities and hurdles that can be created and overcome through WEB 3 technology.
Team
Our team, consisting of four talented people. This project was created as part of a module we are taking at the Lucerne University of Applied Sciences and Arts. We are studying Digital Ideation, three of us majoring in Computer Science and one majoring in Design. This makes us extremely versatile and we can ideally use our mixed skills for projects like this. We are committed to the core values of our society and our environment. That's why we want this site to educate and explain the NFT world a little better.
